Our Mission Statement

Our only goal is to produce tasty coffee is the most eco-friendly way. We also aim to work alongside our coffee bean farmers to help them grow and prosper. You can trust when you get a cup of coffee from Blaze Coffee it was made with the same passion and love its made with by you!

How It All Started

Blaze coffee was founded by our founders Conor and Kirsty, two people from Dublin who have always had a passion for finding the best tasting coffee. While on a 6 month long trip around South America they landed in Columbia where they visited some of the worlds best coffee bean farms. They were blown away with the quality of the coffee they tasted and the idea hit them. Lets bring this world class coffee back to Dublin and when they returned home Blaze Coffee was born.

Getting Off The Ground

They stayed in touch with our Columbian coffee bean farmers and within the week they had fresh beans back in Dublin. Conor and Kirsty spent months perfecting the best way to roast and brew these delicious beans, and after a lot of trial and error they felt the had the perfect tasting coffee. We opened their mobile coffee shop in 2020 which has now grown into their own coffee shop in Rush, Co. Dublin.

Trying To Brew The Best

There is a huge problem in the coffee industry in Dublin. Cheaper coffee means farmers getting paid less for their beans. Thats why we have promised our farmers they will be fairly paid for all the coffee we buy from them. This is why when you buy a coffee thats slightly more expensive than everywhere else, you know that money is going straight back to the source where the farmers can use it to grow even more of their delicious coffee for you.

Helping the Planet

We have also made it our mission to brew our coffee in the most eco-friendly way possible. Our coffee cups are 100% recyclable and bio-degradeable. We also ecourage our customers to bring their own travel mugs for even less waste. Our Shop is also powered 75% by solar power fromt he panels we have installed on top of our shop. We also collect, filter and distill as much rain water as we can so you know your great tasting cup of coffee is helping as much of the planet as it can.
